Stew 1

Now without extra water

What's in it

  • 2 boxes of white mushrooms
  • 2 summer squashes, one big one small
  • 2 bags of radishes, cut randomly for texture
  • 1 bag of small yams
  • 4 sweet potatoes
  • 1 leek (most of it)
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1 pack of sweet italian susage
  • 1 pack of stirfry beef, cubed
  • 2 packs of stew beef
  • 1 pack of hickory smoked backon, cut up as you want
  • 1/2 of a small bag of jasmine rice
  • all purpose flour
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 12 twists of salt from a grinder
  • 15 twists of black pepper from a grinder
  • 2 small boxes of vegetable stock
  • 1 large box of chicken stock
  • 3 cans of yuengling

Prep / cooking instructions

  • cut up and add all the vegetables
  • cut up and add all the meat
  • pour rice on top
  • add the vegetable stock
  • add 2 cans of beer
  • add chicken stock
  • cover stew in medium dusting of flour
  • add salt, pepper, and bay leaves
  • add last beer
  • cook on medium low heat (4 on my stove) for 7 hours, dredging every hour

Rating and comments

Rating: 6/10

Comments:

Overall pretty good stew. Didn't have my spices with me so on the bland side. May be too salty due to premade broth. Squash doesn't really add anything to it.

Stew 2

Breakfast Stew?

What's in it

  • 1 box of white mushrooms
  • 1 medium summer squash
  • 4 sweet potatoes
  • 1/2 Large leek
  • 3 clove of garlic
  • 1 kielbasa
  • 1 pack of fatty beef, cubed
  • 2 packs of pork loin
  • 2 packs of porkchops
  • 1/2lb white rice
  • 2 bay leaves
  • thin layer of paprika
  • dusting of thyme
  • dusting of rosemary
  • 1/2 pack of Japanese White Curry instant stock
  • 6 twists of salt from a grinder
  • 6 twists of black pepper from a grinder
  • 2 small boxes of turkey stock
  • 1/2 gallon apple cider
  • 3 shots of honey whiskey

Prep / cooking instructions

  • cut up the leek and add as first layer
  • slice squash and sweet potatoes, add as second layer
  • add bay leaves
  • cut up and add all the meat
  • completely cover meat with paprika
  • half the mushrooms, then add them to stew
  • slice all garlic, add to stew
  • add rice
  • add salt, pepper, rosemary, and thyme
  • add turkey stock
  • add whiskey
  • break up curry block into small chunks, then add to stew
  • add apple cider
  • cover pot, and cook on medium low (4 on my stove) until simmering
  • cook on low heat (2 on my stove) for 5 hours, dredging every hour

Rating and comments

Rating: 8/10

Comments:

Very applely base, but the flavors of the individual ingredients shine through. Fairly thick consistency overall. Still could use more salt and pepper.

Stew 3

Big Soup

What's in it

  • 1.5 lbs pork bones
  • 1.5 lbs Stew Beef
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 bell pepper, large
  • 1 yellow onion, medium
  • 3 celery stalks, large
  • 3 tsp garlic
  • 2 packs Udon Noodles
  • Fresh rosemary, 4 stalks
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Spicy Chili powder
  • Cuman
  • Water

Prep / cooking instructions

  • brown stew beef in pot
  • remove beef once browned, add water to deglaze
  • peel and rough chop onion, add to water
  • add pork bones to water, cover pork bones with water until water is about 1/2inch above bones
  • add rosemary stalks, add ~15 twists of salt & pepper each
  • bring to boil, then reduce heat to medium low and let simmer for 1.5 hours
  • remove pork bones, remove meat from bones, re-add meat to stock
  • add stew meat, increase heat to medium and cook for 15 minutes
  • wash and chop celery, carrots, and bell pepper, add to stock, cook for 5 minutes
  • add udon noodles, garlic, ~20 twists of salt & pepper
  • add ~2 shakes of cuman, ~2 shakes of Spicy Chili powder
  • cook for 8 minutes
  • remove from heat

Rating and comments

Rating: 6.5/10

Comments:

Ingredients shine through, but the broth is weak. Broth has light mouthfeel and hints of spice and savory flavors.

Ingredients have hints of the added spices as well as their normal flavors. Carrots, celery, and peppers crunchy (which is good).
