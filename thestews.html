Stew 1

Now without extra water

What's in it

  • 2 boxes of white mushrooms
  • 2 summer squashes, one big one small
  • 2 bags of radishes, cut randomly for texture
  • 1 bag of small yams
  • 4 sweet potatoes
  • 1 leek (most of it)
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1 pack of sweet italian susage
  • 1 pack of stirfry beef, cubed
  • 2 packs of stew beef
  • 1 pack of hickory smoked backon, cut up as you want
  • 1/2 of a small bag of jasmine rice
  • all purpose flour
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 12 twists of salt from a grinder
  • 15 twists of black pepper from a grinder
  • 2 small boxes of vegetable stock
  • 1 large box of chicken stock
  • 3 cans of yuengling

Prep / cooking instructions

  • cut up and add all the vegetables
  • cut up and add all the meat
  • pour rice on top
  • add the vegetable stock
  • add 2 cans of beer
  • add chicken stock
  • cover stew in medium dusting of flour
  • add salt, pepper, and bay leaves
  • add last beer
  • cook on medium low heat (4 on my stove) for 7 hours, dredging every hour

Rating and comments

Rating: 6/10

Comments:

Overall pretty good stew. Didn't have my spices with me so on the bland side. May be too salty due to premade broth. Squash doesn't really add anything to it.
