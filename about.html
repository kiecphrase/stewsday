Why Stew?

I had a 5 gallon pot, a lot of stuff about to go bad in my refrigerator, and all the time in the world.

What is stew?

No idea what the actual definition is, but I feel like you know it when you eat it.

In my opinion, stew is a soup that you have to think about when you eat it. It doesn't have to be hot, it just has to be a little thicker than a true liquid, or maybe too dense to be considered a starter.

Why document your stew journey?

I can never remember my recipes so I had to do something to remember my past and make my stew future better.
